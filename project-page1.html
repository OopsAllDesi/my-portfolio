Content for Vice

In semester 3 of my studies our class had Vice Media as a client and we had to come up with a concept for them. Me and my group focused on mental health awareness. We came up with a video, explaining the effects of eating disorders.

Role: Screenwriter

Mental Playground

For the client Scenery, my team’s project was an interactive movie about a person with BPD. In this movie, the viewer has control over how the story goes. If chosen right, the story continues and you help the character deal with his symptoms, if not, the story starts again. (press the youtube suggested video - start - that pops up in the end to continue the story)

Role: Director, Screenwriter, Second Cameraman, Set Design
