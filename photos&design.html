Design Projects

Social Media Designs

Post I have made for the official ISA Instagram account

As part of the Marketing team of the International Student Association I had the task to run the Instagram account. One of my key responsibilities was to design promotional posters for upcoming events, such as social gatherings, workshops, and our major activities like game nights and charity tournaments. I then shared these materials through the Instagram platform, using captions, stories, and scheduling strategies to maximize visibility and participation .

I remained on this position for the duration of my 2nd year at NHL Stenden, contributing to the ISA's growth and helping foster a more connected and engaged international student network.

Journal and Magazine

The more cretive approach

In the beginning of my second semester at NHL I received my first ever design task. I had to make a magazine depicting me as an individual. It was mandatory to use Adobe Illustrator. Back then, it was my first time using Illustrator but I did everything by myself – from the design to the photos

As time progressed, new assignments arose. For one of them we had to develop an idea and deliver different media products each week for a couple of weeks. My idea was revolving around a mentally ill young girl who killed her brother. Since my first product was from 1st POV, I decided to make a journal to expose the character’s inner thoughts and to show how the illness took over time .

Brochures and Menus
